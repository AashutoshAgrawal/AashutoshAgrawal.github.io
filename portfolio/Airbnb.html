My Presentation

Slide 1: Introduction

Content Overview: This slide introduces TikTok Shop as a dynamic e-commerce platform that connects sellers with a large, active user base.

Key Statistics:Sellers on TikTok Shop have experienced up to 300% sales growth within their first month of joining. The platform boasts over 110,000 active daily buyers, demonstrating its robust market presence and potential.

Purpose: To showcase the significant opportunities TikTok Shop offers to sellers looking to expand their reach and revenue.

Identifying the Gap

This slide identifies the challenges faced by TikTok Shop sellers in analyzing customer feedback and reviews. Sellers face difficulties in:

  • Prioritizing critical feedback over less impactful comments.
  • Making sense of subjective reviews and lengthy customer feedback.
  • Lack of tools for direct customer follow-up and feedback resolution, leading to unresolved issues and customer dissatisfaction.

Proposed Orientation

An AI-powered system is proposed to analyze customer reviews and feedback, providing actionable insights for sellers. The AI system will help sellers identify and prioritize areas for improvement and offer tailored recommendations to enhance products and services.

Impact of the Solution

The AI system will provide several benefits:

  • Identify improvement areas and enhance product quality.
  • Improve operational efficiency by enabling more effective feedback processing.
  • Increase customer satisfaction, reduce refund rates, and foster long-term growth.

User Pain Points

Sellers currently face multiple pain points:

  • Lack of sentiment clarity in current post-purchase analytics dashboards.
  • Difficulty in differentiating between high-impact concerns (like product quality) and less critical feedback (such as packaging preferences).
  • Overwhelming and unclear feedback that makes it hard to prioritize actionable insights.
  • Inability to address feedback proactively, resulting in unresolved issues and missed opportunities for trust-building.

Current vs. Proposed Features

The current system lacks advanced analytics tools for sentiment analysis and actionable insights. The proposed solution will:

  • Leverage AI to analyze feedback and sentiment, offering clear recommendations.
  • Provide tools for real-time feedback resolution, improving customer satisfaction.
